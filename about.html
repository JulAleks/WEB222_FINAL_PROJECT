About

Julia is an aspiring programming student with a keen interest in blending technicality and art. She possesses a strong background in Graphic and Interactive Media Design and has previously collaborated with various platforms, showcasing her creative skills and innovative thinking. Additionally, she has a passion for User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) design, aiming to create seamless and enjoyable digital experiences for users.

Despite spending the last decade actively involved in the healthcare industry after relocating to Canada, Julia is determined to make a comeback to the world of technology. She is actively working on improving her programming skills through formal education, eager to apply her design expertise in the tech domain.

Alongside her technical pursuits, Julia loves the card game TAKI, which she has enjoyed playing with friends since childhood and continues to cherish as a source of fun and social connection.

Looking ahead, Julia is eager to explore collaborative opportunities to develop innovative solutions that bridge the gap between design and technology. Her blend of creativity, technical know-how, and passion for UI/UX makes her a promising candidate for projects that require an artistic touch and a focus on user-centered design.
